# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Tanaghom is used by business owners, campaign operators, content reviewers, and
sales leaders coordinating AI-assisted content-to-sales work. They need to
understand what agents are doing, resolve blocked work, approve public-facing
content, and measure campaign and sales progress without entering n8n.

The primary workflow is operational: open the product, identify what requires
attention, make informed approval decisions, and verify that automated work is
progressing safely.

## Product Purpose

Tanaghom provides one trustworthy control surface for four cooperating business
agents: Campaign Strategist, Content Producer, Publisher and Performance
Monitor, and Sales and CRM Agent. It makes agent work visible, keeps PostgreSQL
as the source of truth, and ensures that public content cannot bypass human
approval.

Success means an owner can run a staged campaign from brief through strategy,
content review, publishing, lead capture, and sales reporting while retaining a
complete explanation of every automated action.

## Brand Personality

Calm, intelligent, operational. Tanaghom should feel precise and capable without
feeling cold, theatrical, or machine-obsessed. Its voice is direct, reassuring,
and specific about state, risk, and next actions.

## Anti-references

- Neon or high-saturation “AI” styling.
- Cartoon robots, anthropomorphic agents, and chat-bubble-first interfaces.
- Sci-fi command centers, glowing dashboards, or decorative dark-mode effects.
- Glassmorphism, excessive gradients, oversized metric cards, and ornamental
  motion.
- Dense enterprise software that hides priorities behind tables and menus.

## Design Principles

1. **Attention before analytics.** Lead with decisions, blockers, and work that
   needs a human; supporting metrics come second.
2. **Explain every state.** Agent status, campaign progress, and failures always
   include a reason, owner, timestamp, and appropriate next action.
3. **Approval is a deliberate act.** Review surfaces show enough context for a
   confident decision and make rejection feedback constructive and traceable.
4. **Familiar controls, distinctive composition.** Standard product affordances
   remain predictable while layout and hierarchy give Tanaghom its own identity.
5. **Progressive operational depth.** Executives can scan quickly; operators can
   drill into jobs, events, and audit evidence without leaving the product.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA across desktop and responsive mobile web. Support keyboard-only
operation, visible focus, reduced motion, non-color state cues, readable data
contrast, and touch targets suitable for mobile use. Build layout, components,
and content direction with future Arabic localization and RTL mirroring in mind
from the beginning.
